Data Download

The HTAN Portal leverages several data repositories to provide download of data:
  • Synapse - Dowload processed level 3 and level 4 data
  • dbGaP - Download Level 1 & 2 access controlled data
  • Imaging Data Commons (IDC) - Download imaging data in DICOM-TIFF format
Instructions for downloading from these repositories can be found directly from the Explore Page. by (1) making a selection of files and clicking on the download button or (2) by going to a publication page and clicking on the download button there.
