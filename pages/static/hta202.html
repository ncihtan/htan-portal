Understanding the Role of Tumor Microenvironment in Low-Grade Glioma Progression to Malignancy

Grant Number: CA294551
Principal Investigators:

  • Long Cai, CalTech (Contact PI)
  • Richard Everson, UCLA
  • Matthew Thomson, CalTech
  • Barbara Wold, CalTech
This project focuses on creating a spatial atlas of low-grade gliomas to understand how they progress to more aggressive forms of cancer. By comparing tumors that remain indolent with those that become malignant, the team aims to identify early molecular and cellular changes that predict this progression. Advanced imaging and molecular techniques will be used to map these changes, providing insights into potential therapeutic interventions to prevent malignancy.
