Center for Gastric Pre-Cancer Atlas of Multidimensional Evolution in 3D (GAME3D)

Grant Number: CA294518
Principal Investigators:

  • Linghua Wang, MD Anderson (Contact PI)
  • Paul Mansfield, MD Anderson
  • Tae Hyun Hwang, Mayo Clinic
  • Mingyao Li, University of Pennsylvania
This center aims to map the progression of hereditary diffuse gastric cancer (HDGC), focusing on the transition from early pre-cancerous lesions to advanced gastric cancer. Using advanced 2D and 3D spatial techniques, the research will track cellular and molecular changes in patients with CDH1 mutations to identify early markers of cancer development. The findings will guide more personalized approaches to cancer prevention and treatment, potentially reducing the need for invasive surgeries like prophylactic gastrectomies.
